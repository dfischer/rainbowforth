About Icon Forth

A picture is said to be worth a thousand words. Icon Forth is an experiment in what can happen when traditional Forth words are replaced with images and the dictionary becomes a collaboratively edited online database.

In Icon Forth, a Forth definition like:

: square dup * ;
becomes:
:
Each 16x16 icon, pictographically depicts what the word means, and words are strung together to construct more complex concepts from simpler ones.

Icon Forth, like a wiki, is a collaborative development space. Anyone can add a word, and anyone can use and explorer the words added by others. A User Guide is provided to get you started. It will teach you how to use the Icon Forth Editor to explorer existing words in the global dictionary, or how to create words of your own.
