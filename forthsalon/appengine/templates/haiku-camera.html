Animated Haiku

Some haiku can now use image data from a user camera/webcam. The bwsample word is used to sample grayscale data. The sample word is used to sample RGB data.

These haiku can only capture camera data using Google Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.

The haiku is converted on the fly to a GLSL fragment shader.
