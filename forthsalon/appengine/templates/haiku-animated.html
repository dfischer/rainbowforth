Animated Haiku

Some haiku are now animated using the 't' word. These haiku can only be seen in animated form using Google Chrome, Firefox, and Safari. Click on haiku marked with an 'a' to see the animation.

The haiku is converted on the fly to a GLSL fragment shader.

This feature is a work in progress and may change.
