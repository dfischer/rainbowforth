Animated Haiku

Some haiku are now animated using the t word to parameterize the haiku by time. The dt is also available to base animation on the time since the last frame. When used in tandem with @ and ! it can be used to make stateful time based haiku.

These haiku can only be seen in animated form using Google Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari. Click on haiku marked with an 'ani' to see the animation.

The haiku is converted on the fly to a GLSL fragment shader.
