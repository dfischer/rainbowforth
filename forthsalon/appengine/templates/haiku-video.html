Video Haiku

Some haiku are now textured using a video feed sample and bwsample words to get image samples from a camera in rgb and greyscale.

If video hardware is unavailable, the SVFIG logo is used in place of the video feed.

These haiku can only be seen in animated form using Google Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Safari.

The haiku is converted on the fly to a GLSL fragment shader.
