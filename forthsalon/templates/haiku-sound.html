Haiku Sound

Some haiku now have audio. The sound can only be heard at the moment in browsers that support the WebAudio API. This is a very new API and is not supported in most browsers. This feature works on OSX in 'Stable Channel' Google Chrome. On other platforms is known to work in 'Canary' channel.

Haiku containing the word 'audio' will be treated as two sections. Everything before 'audio' will be a typical graphical haiku. Everything after will be a sound haiku expected to emit a single data stack value for the audio amplitude (-1 thru 1) using 't' as an input. A planned future feature: stereo may be supported by running the haiku twice with 'x' at -1 and 1 for left and right.

This feature is a work in progress and may change.
