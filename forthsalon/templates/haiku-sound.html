Haiku Sound

Audio can now be synthesized to accompany haiku. Click 'Play Audio' / 'Mute Audio' to toggle. The sound can only be heard at the moment in browsers that support the WebAudio API. This is a very new API and is not supported in most browsers. This feature works on mainly in Google Chrome.

Audio is produced by sampling the red component at (0, 0.5) to select amplitude, and the red component at (1, 0.5) to select pitch. Both are clipped to [0, 1]. Pitch is mapped to a ten note pentatonic scale. Fractional pitches produce a mix of neighboring tones on the scale. This general scheme was selected to produce non-cacophonous sounds. Prior attempts with arbitrary audio sounded horrible.

This feature is a work in progress and may change.
